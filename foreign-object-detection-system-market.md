<p><p>The Foreign Object Detection System market comprises three primary types: magnetic separators, metal detectors, and X-ray machines. Magnetic separators utilize magnetic forces to extract ferrous and non-ferrous metals from products, ensuring quality and safety. Metal detectors identify metallic contaminants in various materials, commonly used in food and beverage industries. X-ray machines offer advanced imaging technology to detect a wide range of foreign objects, including plastics and glass, providing a comprehensive solution for quality control in diverse sectors.</p></p>
