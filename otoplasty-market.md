<p><strong>The Otoplasty Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-Helical Fold Manipulation</li><li>Conchal Alteration</li><li>Correction Of Earlobe Prominence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The otoplasty market comprises three primary types: Anti-Helical Fold Manipulation, Conchal Alteration, and Correction of Earlobe Prominence. Anti-Helical Fold Manipulation involves reshaping the outer ear to create a more defined fold, enhancing the ear's contour. Conchal Alteration focuses on reducing the size or prominence of the concha, improving overall ear aesthetics. Correction of Earlobe Prominence addresses protruding earlobes, aligning them closer to the head for a balanced appearance. Each technique aims to enhance ear symmetry and overall facial harmony.</p></p>
