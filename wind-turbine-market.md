<p><p>The wind turbine market is primarily categorized into horizontal-axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical-axis wind turbines (VAWT), along with other niche technologies. HAWTs are the most common type, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, optimizing efficiency at high wind speeds. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages in turbulent wind conditions and lower heights. Additionally, the "other" market encompasses innovative designs, such as offshore and small-scale turbines, catering to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
