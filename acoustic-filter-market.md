<p><strong>The Acoustic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic filter market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters utilize surface waves on piezoelectric materials, making them suitable for applications requiring low frequency and broadband performance, particularly in mobile communication devices. BAW filters, on the other hand, exploit bulk waves, offering superior performance at higher frequencies, which is ideal for advanced applications like 5G communications and high-frequency wireless systems. Both technologies cater to evolving wireless demands.</p></p>
