<p><p>The die-casting peripheral automation equipment market includes various specialized machines that enhance efficiency and productivity in manufacturing processes. Unloading machines remove finished products from molds, while spraying machines apply lubricants to prevent sticking. Ladling machines transport molten metal to molds, and sprue removal machines cut off excess material post-casting. Robot automatic peripheral configuration devices integrate robotic systems for seamless operation. Other market components may encompass equipment like mold temperature controllers and coolant systems, all aimed at optimizing die-casting operations.</p></p>
