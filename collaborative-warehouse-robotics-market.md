<p><p>The collaborative warehouse robotics market is primarily divided into two types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics includes autonomous robots that navigate through warehouse spaces, facilitating transport and sorting tasks without the need for fixed tracks. In contrast, fixed robotics refers to stationary robotic systems designed for specific tasks, such as picking, packing, and palletizing. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve workflow, enabling warehouses to adapt to varying demands and streamline processes seamlessly.</p></p>
