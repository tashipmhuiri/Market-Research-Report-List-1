<p><p>The aluminum foil coil market consists of three main types: hard foil, semi-hard foil, and soft foil. Hard foil is characterized by its rigidity and tear resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring structural integrity, such as packaging and insulation. Semi-hard foil offers a balance between flexibility and strength, ideal for food packaging and household products. Soft foil, being highly flexible, is used in various applications including wrapping and cooking, providing excellent conformability and sealing properties.</p></p>
