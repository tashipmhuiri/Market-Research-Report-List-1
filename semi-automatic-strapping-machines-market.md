<p><p>The semi-automatic strapping machine market encompasses two main types: welding strapping machines and fastening strapping machines. Welding strapping machines utilize heat to fuse the strapping material, providing a strong bond suitable for securing heavier loads. In contrast, fastening strapping machines employ plastic buckles or clips to secure the straps without heat, offering flexibility and ease of operation. Both types are designed to enhance packaging efficiency, improve load stability, and streamline processes in various industries, including logistics and manufacturing.</p></p>
