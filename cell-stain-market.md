<p><p>The **Cell Stain Market** includes **Acidic Cell Stains**, **Basic Cell Stains**, and **Neutral Cell Stains**, each serving distinct purposes in biological research and diagnostics. **Acidic Cell Stains** target basic cellular components like cytoplasm, using negatively charged dyes. **Basic Cell Stains** bind to acidic structures such as nucleic acids, utilizing positively charged dyes. **Neutral Cell Stains** combine both acidic and basic dyes to stain diverse cellular structures simultaneously. These stains are vital for visualizing cellular morphology, differentiation, and pathology in microscopy and histology applications.</p></p>
