<p><p>The external broaching machines market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal broaching machines. Vertical broaching machines typically feature a vertical workpiece orientation, allowing for efficient cutting and finishing of components. They are ideal for thicker materials and complex shapes. In contrast, horizontal broaching machines position the workpiece horizontally, providing a stable platform for longer parts and high production rates. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, offering various advantages based on design and operational requirements.</p></p>
