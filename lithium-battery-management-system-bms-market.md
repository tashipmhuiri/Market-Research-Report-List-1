<p><p>A Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) is essential for monitoring and managing battery performance, ensuring safety, and optimizing lifespan. The market is segmented into two types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS utilizes a single control unit to monitor all cells, simplifying design and reducing costs, but can have limitations in scalability and fault tolerance. On the other hand, Distributed BMS employs multiple control units, enhancing scalability and resilience, making it ideal for larger applications like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
