<p><strong>The Semiconductor Testing Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ProbeCard</li><li>LoadBoard</li><li>Burn-inBoard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor testing board market includes three main types: Probe Cards, Load Boards, and Burn-in Boards. Probe Cards are used for initial testing of semiconductor wafers by connecting probes to the chip pads. Load Boards facilitate the functional testing of packaged chips by interfacing with the tester and ensuring proper electrical connections. Burn-in Boards are designed for reliability testing under stress conditions, exposing semiconductors to extreme temperatures and voltages to identify potential failures early in the lifecycle.</p></p>
