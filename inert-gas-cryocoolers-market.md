<p><strong>The Inert Gas Cryocoolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inert gas cryocoolers are categorized into two main types: closed loop and open loop systems. Closed loop cryocoolers recirculate the inert gas within a sealed system, maintaining consistent performance and efficiency while minimizing gas loss. In contrast, open loop cryocoolers introduce fresh inert gas into the system while expelling used gas, resulting in simpler designs but potentially higher operational costs due to gas consumption. Both systems are employed in applications requiring low temperatures, offering unique advantages based on the specific requirements.</p></p>
