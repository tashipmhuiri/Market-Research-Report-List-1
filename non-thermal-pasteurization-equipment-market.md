<p><p>Non-thermal pasteurization equipment encompasses various technologies that enhance food safety and shelf life without traditional heat. High Pressure Processing (HPP) utilizes intense pressure to kill pathogens while preserving flavor and nutrients. Pulse Electric Field (PEF applies short bursts of electricity to disrupt microbial cells. Microwave Volumetric Heating (MVH) uses microwaves to heat food from within. Irradiation exposes food to ionizing radiation to eliminate harmful organisms. Ultrasonic technology employs sound waves for microbial reduction, while "Others" may include emerging or niche technologies in this category.</p></p>
