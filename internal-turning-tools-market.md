<p><p>The internal turning tools market consists of various specialized tools designed for specific machining operations. Grooving tools create narrow grooves or slots in materials, enhancing precision and profiles. Threading tools cut internal threads, essential for fastening applications. End milling tools are versatile, allowing for various cuts within a workpiece for shapes and contours. Drilling tools create internal holes, providing necessary access points or passageways. Each type plays a critical role in precision engineering and manufacturing across diverse industries.</p></p>
