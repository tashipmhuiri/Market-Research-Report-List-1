<p><ul><li>Arc Welding Machine</li><li>Gas Welding/Cutting Machine</li><li>Resistance Welding Machine</li><li>Laser Welding Machine</li><li>Ultrasonic Welding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding machinery market consists of various types, each serving distinct applications. Arc welding machines use electric arcs to melt metals, ideal for heavy-duty fabrication. Gas welding/cutting machines employ flame for welding and cutting metals, suitable for lighter tasks. Resistance welding machines join materials through heat generated by electric resistance, commonly used in automotive manufacturing. Laser welding machines utilize focused laser beams for precision and speed, effective in intricate designs. Ultrasonic welding machines apply high-frequency vibrations to bond materials, often used in plastics and microelectronics.</p></p>
