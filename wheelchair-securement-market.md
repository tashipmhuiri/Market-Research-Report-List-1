<p><ul><li>Non-Retractable</li><li>Retractable</li><li>Docking System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair securement market comprises three main types: non-retractable, retractable, and docking systems. Non-retractable systems involve fixed straps or anchors that are permanently installed in vehicles, ensuring secure positioning without flexibility. Retractable systems feature adjustable straps that can be extended or retracted as needed, offering convenience and ease of use. Docking systems allow for quick and efficient wheelchair attachment using a fixed base in a vehicle, providing a stable and secure environment for passengers while accommodating various wheelchair designs.</p></p>
