<p><p>The reactive emulsifier market is primarily categorized into two types: anionic and nonionic emulsifiers. Anionic emulsifiers carry a negative charge, aiding in stabilizing oil-in-water emulsions and are commonly used in personal care products and coatings. Nonionic reactive emulsifiers, on the other hand, do not carry any charge, offering versatility in various applications such as pharmaceuticals and food products. Both types enhance product stability and performance, catering to diverse industrial needs and formulations.</p></p>
