<p><p>The commercial masticating juicer market features two primary types: vertical auger and horizontal auger juicers. Vertical auger juicers utilize a vertically oriented auger that efficiently extracts juice by grinding and pressing fruits and vegetables, optimizing space and processing speed. In contrast, horizontal auger juicers have a horizontally aligned auger that excels in handling leafy greens and dense produce while providing high juice yield. Both types cater to commercial needs, offering durability and performance for high-volume juicing operations.</p></p>
