<p><p>The vinyl pipe market includes various types such as PE (Polyethylene), PU (Polyurethane), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and others. PE pipes are flexible, durable, and resistant to chemicals, making them ideal for water and gas distribution. PU pipes are lightweight, abrasion-resistant, and used in applications requiring flexibility, like pneumatic systems. PVC pipes are rigid, cost-effective, and widely used in plumbing, drainage, and irrigation. Other vinyl pipes may include specialized materials for unique applications, offering tailored properties like enhanced strength, temperature resistance, or chemical compatibility.</p></p>
