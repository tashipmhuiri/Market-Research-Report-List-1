<p><strong>The Dental Allograft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Gingival Graft</li><li>Connective Tissue Graft</li><li>Pedicle Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental allograft market comprises various graft types, including Free Gingival Graft, Connective Tissue Graft, and Pedicle Graft. Free Gingival Grafts are used to enhance the width of keratinized tissue, improving gum health. Connective Tissue Grafts involve harvesting tissue from the palate to cover exposed roots, promoting esthetics and sensitivity relief. Pedicle Grafts utilize adjacent gum tissue, allowing for immediate integration without significant recovery time. Each graft type addresses specific periodontal needs, facilitating effective dental procedures and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
