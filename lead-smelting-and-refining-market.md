<p><p>Lead smelting and refining encompass various market types aimed at extracting lead from ores and recycling materials. The pyrometallurgical method involves high-temperature processes to separate lead from impurities. Solvent extraction employs chemical solvents for selective separation and purification of lead from other metals. Ion exchange involves passing a solution through a medium that selectively adsorbs lead ions, effectively concentrating them for recovery. Each method is tailored to optimize efficiency, purity, and environmental sustainability in lead production and recycling.</p></p>
