<p><p>The window regulators market is primarily divided into two types: electric and manual window regulators. Electric window regulators utilize motors to automate window operations, offering convenience and ease of use, which makes them popular in modern vehicles. Manual window regulators, on the other hand, rely on a hand-operated crank or lever, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution, often found in older or budget models. Each type caters to different consumer preferences regarding functionality, price, and vehicle design.</p></p>
