<p><p>The nanoparticle size measuring instrument market primarily includes Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS) and laser-based systems. Laser-based systems, on the other hand, involve techniques like laser diffraction, offering rapid and precise measurements for larger particles across solid and liquid samples. Both types contribute significantly to advancing research in fields like pharmaceuticals, nanotechnology, and materials science.</p></p>
