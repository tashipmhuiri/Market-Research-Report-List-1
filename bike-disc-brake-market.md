<p><p>The bike disc brake market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and hydraulic disc brakes. In contrast, hydraulic disc brakes use fluid pressure to provide more powerful and consistent braking performance, making them favored by competitive cyclists and mountain bikers who require precise control and better modulation on diverse terrains.</p></p>
