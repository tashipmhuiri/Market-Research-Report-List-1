<p><p>The marble facade cladding market is divided into two main types: engineered marble and natural marble. Engineered marble is composed of crushed marble stones mixed with resin, offering uniformity in color and texture, and increased durability. It is lightweight and often more cost-effective. Natural marble, on the other hand, is quarried directly from the earth, showcasing unique patterns and organic beauty. It is valued for its authenticity and timeless appeal, though it may be heavier and more expensive.</p></p>
