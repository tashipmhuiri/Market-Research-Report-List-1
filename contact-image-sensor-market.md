<p><p>The Contact Image Sensor (CIS) market comprises two primary types: monochrome and color contact image sensors. Monochrome contact image sensors capture images in shades of gray, making them ideal for applications requiring high contrast and fine detail, such as scanning text documents and barcodes. In contrast, color contact image sensors can capture a full spectrum of colors, making them suitable for applications like photographic scanning and color document reproduction. Both types are crucial in industries such as printing, medical imaging, and automation.</p></p>
