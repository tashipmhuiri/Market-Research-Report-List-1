<p><p>The EDM cutting wire market comprises three main types: No Coated Wire, Coated Wire, and Hybrid Wire. No Coated Wire features a bare surface, offering high purity and conductivity, suitable for specific applications. Coated Wire has a protective layer, enhancing durability and reducing wear, making it ideal for abrasive environments. Hybrid Wire combines both features, providing versatility and improved performance in various machining conditions. Each type serves distinct needs within industries reliant on electrical discharge machining.</p></p>
