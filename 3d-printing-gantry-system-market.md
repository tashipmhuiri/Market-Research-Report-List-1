<p><p>The 3D printing gantry system market is categorized into three primary types: XY, XYZ, and others. The XY gantry system features movement on a two-dimensional plane, suitable for basic printing applications. The XYZ system adds vertical movement, allowing for more complex geometries and enhanced printing capabilities. Other systems may include Delta or CoreXY designs, which offer unique advantages like speed and efficiency. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ranging from prototyping to advanced manufacturing processes.</p></p>
