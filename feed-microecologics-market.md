<p><p>The feed microbiologics market encompasses three primary types: probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live microorganisms that enhance gut health and improve animal performance. Prebiotics are substances that promote the growth of beneficial gut bacteria, thereby supporting overall digestive health. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics, maximizing their benefits for animal nutrition. Together, these components contribute to improved feed efficiency, better immunity, and enhanced overall well-being of livestock, driving growth in the animal feed industry.</p></p>
