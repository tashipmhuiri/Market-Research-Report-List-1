<p><p>Non-invasive blood pressure monitors are categorized primarily into digital and mechanical types. Digital monitors utilize advanced technology, offering automatic readings and features like memory storage and connectivity to smartphones, providing ease of use and accuracy. Mechanical monitors, often utilizing a sphygmomanometer and stethoscope, require manual operation and interpretation, making them more traditional in approach. Both types serve the essential purpose of measuring blood pressure without penetrating the skin, catering to diverse user preferences and clinical settings.</p></p>
