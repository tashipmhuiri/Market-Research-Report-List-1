<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-contained Power Supply</li><li>Centralized Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency lighting market comprises two main types: self-contained power supply and centralized power supply systems. Self-contained units are standalone fixtures equipped with their own battery, providing reliable illumination during power outages. These are ideal for smaller installations or areas with limited infrastructure. In contrast, centralized power supply systems use a central battery source to power multiple fixtures across a facility, offering more extensive coverage and easier maintenance but requiring more complex wiring and setup.</p></p>
