<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convoluted Type</li><li>Sleeve Type</li><li>Rolling Lobe Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle air spring market consists of three main types: convoluted, sleeve, and rolling lobe air springs. Convoluted air springs feature a bellows design that allows for significant deflection and is ideal for heavy loads, providing excellent ride quality. Sleeve air springs are characterized by a tubular shape that offers linear movement and flexibility, suitable for various applications. Rolling lobe air springs utilize a rolling motion to minimize friction, enhancing durability and performance, especially in demanding environments.</p></p>
