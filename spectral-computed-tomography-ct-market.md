<p><strong>The Spectral Computed Tomography (CT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral CT</li><li>Electron Beam Tomography</li><li>Multi-Slice Computed Tomography</li><li>Positron Emission Tomography (Pet)</li><li>Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectral Computed Tomography (CT) encompasses various imaging techniques that enhance diagnostic capabilities. Spiral CT utilizes a continuous rotation, providing detailed cross-sectional images rapidly. Electron Beam Tomography excels in cardiac imaging through fast, high-resolution scans. Multi-Slice Computed Tomography offers multiple slices per rotation, improving speed and detail. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) reveals metabolic processes, while Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) assesses functional imaging. Together, these modalities drive advancements in precision medicine and personalized diagnostics across diverse clinical applications.</p></p>
