<p><strong>The RF Feeder Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coax or Coaxial Cable</li><li>Open Wire or Twin Feeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF feeder cables market consists of two main types: coaxial cables and open wire (twin feeder) cables. Coaxial cables feature a central conductor surrounded by insulation and a conductive shield, making them ideal for low-loss signal transmission in compact spaces. They are widely used in television and radio broadcasting. Open wire or twin feeders consist of two parallel conductors, offering lower attenuation and higher power handling, making them suitable for high-frequency applications in telecommunication and broadcasting.</p></p>
