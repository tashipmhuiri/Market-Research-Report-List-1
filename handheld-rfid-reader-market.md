<p><strong>The Handheld RFID Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>High Frequency Handheld RFID readers</li><li>Ultra High Frequency handheld RFID readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld RFID reader market is divided into three main types based on frequency. Low-frequency handheld RFID readers operate typically at 125 kHz to 134 kHz, making them suitable for animal tracking and access control. High-frequency handheld RFID readers function at 13.56 MHz, widely used for inventory management and smart cards. Ultra-high frequency handheld RFID readers, operating from 860 MHz to 960 MHz, provide longer read ranges and faster data transfer, ideal for supply chain management and logistics applications.</p></p>
