<p><p>The GMO test kits market comprises various types, notably PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay). PCR is used for detecting genetically modified organisms by amplifying specific DNA sequences, ensuring high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, ELISA relies on antigen-antibody interactions to quantify GMO proteins, offering a straightforward and rapid analysis. Both methods cater to regulatory compliance and food safety, enabling industries to monitor and control GMO content in agricultural products effectively.</p></p>
