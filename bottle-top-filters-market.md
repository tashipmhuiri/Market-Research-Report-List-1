<p><p>Bottle top filters are crucial in laboratory applications for sample preparation and sterilization. The market can be divided into three main types: </p><p>1. **With SFCA Membrane**: These utilize a surfactant-free cellulose acetate membrane, ideal for filtering biological samples due to their superior biocompatibility and low protein binding.</p><p>2. **With PES Membrane**: Polyethersulfone membranes are favored for their higher flow rates and chemical resistance, making them suitable for a wide range of solutions.</p><p>3. **Others**: This category includes various membranes like Nylon and PTFE, catering to specific filtration needs.</p></p>
