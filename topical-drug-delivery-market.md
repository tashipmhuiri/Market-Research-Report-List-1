<p><ul><li>Transdermal Drug Delivery Devices</li><li>Topical Drug Delivery Formulations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The topical drug delivery market comprises two main types: transdermal drug delivery devices and topical drug delivery formulations. Transdermal devices, such as patches, deliver drugs through the skin into the bloodstream, ensuring systemic effects and patient compliance. In contrast, topical formulations include creams, gels, and ointments designed for local effects, targeting skin ailments or localized conditions. Both types enhance therapeutic efficacy while minimizing systemic side effects, making them integral in the management of various health conditions.</p></p>
