<p><p>The probe card market is categorized into three main types: Cantilever Probe Cards, Vertical Probe Cards, and MEMS Probe Cards. Cantilever Probe Cards utilize a simple, mechanical design suitable for various applications, offering cost-effectiveness and reliability. Vertical Probe Cards feature a straight vertical alignment that enhances test accuracy and minimizes space, making them ideal for high-density configurations. MEMS Probe Cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology, providing enhanced performance and versatility for advanced semiconductor testing applications, facilitating higher precision and adaptability.</p></p>
