<p><ul><li>Side Entry</li><li>Rear Entry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair accessible van market primarily features two types: side entry and rear entry vehicles. Side entry vans have a lowered floor with a ramp that extends from the side, allowing easy access for wheelchair users and facilitating maneuverability in tighter spaces. Rear entry vans feature ramps that deploy from the back, offering a more discreet look and potentially more cargo space inside. Both designs cater to different needs and preferences, enhancing mobility options for individuals with disabilities.</p></p>
