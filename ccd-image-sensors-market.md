<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Full Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Frame Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD image sensor market features various types, each designed for specific applications. Frame Transfer CCD sensors capture images quickly by using a dedicated area for storage, ideal for high-speed imaging. Full Frame Transfer CCD sensors utilize the entire sensor area for both capturing and storing images, enhancing light sensitivity. Interline Transfer CCD sensors allow simultaneous readout and exposure by interleaving storage and imaging cells, suited for video applications. Frame Interline Transfer CCD sensors combine these techniques, achieving high-quality imaging with faster readout speeds.</p></p>
