<p><strong>The Natural Rubber Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural rubber adhesives market primarily includes two key types: solvent-based adhesives and waterborne adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents for application, offering strong bonding and quick drying times, making them suitable for industrial use. Waterborne adhesives, on the other hand, use water as a solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound emissions, which are more environmentally friendly. Both types are widely employed across various industries, including automotive, construction, and packaging, due to their effective bonding properties and versatility.</p></p>
