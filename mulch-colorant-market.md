<p><p>The mulch colorant market offers two primary types: carbon-based dyes and iron oxide-based dyes. Carbon-based dyes are derived from organic materials, providing vibrant colors and are often environmentally friendly. They enhance aesthetic appeal while decomposing naturally. In contrast, iron oxide-based dyes are mineral-based, known for their durability and resistance to fading. They deliver rich, long-lasting colors and are commonly used in landscaping applications for their stability and non-toxicity. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences in garden and landscape design.</p></p>
