<p><p>The microbial soil inoculants market encompasses three primary types: bacterial, fungal, and composite inoculants. Bacterial inoculants utilize beneficial bacteria to enhance soil fertility and plant growth by fixing nitrogen or degrading organic matter. Fungal inoculants, primarily mycorrhizal fungi, improve nutrient uptake and soil structure. Composite inoculants combine both bacterial and fungal components, offering synergistic benefits for soil health and crop productivity. This market is driven by the rising demand for sustainable agriculture practices and improved crop yields.</p></p>
