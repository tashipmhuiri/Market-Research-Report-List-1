<p><p>Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) is a technology that streamlines the process of dispatching emergency services and managing incidents. The market for CAD systems is primarily divided into two types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises CAD is installed locally on an organization's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based CAD is hosted remotely, offering scalability, easier maintenance, and accessibility from various locations, which is increasingly appealing for modern emergency response needs.</p></p>
