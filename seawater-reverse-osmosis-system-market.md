<p><p>The seawater reverse osmosis (SWRO) system market is categorized into one-way and two-way reverse osmosis systems. One-way reverse osmosis systems expel concentrated brine and only allow freshwater to pass, making them efficient for desalination. Two-way reverse osmosis systems, in contrast, can manage both freshwater extraction and brine processing, enabling more flexibility in water management. Each type serves distinct needs in water treatment, addressing varying challenges in desalination and resource sustainability in coastal areas.</p></p>
