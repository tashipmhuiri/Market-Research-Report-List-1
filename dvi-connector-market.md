<p><strong>The DVI Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The DVI connector market is segmented into three main types: DVI-A, DVI-D, and DVI-I. DVI-A (Analog) supports only analog signals, making it suitable for older displays. DVI-D (Digital) transmits only digital signals, ideal for modern digital monitors. DVI-I (Integrative) combines both analog and digital capabilities, providing versatility for various display types. Each type caters to different consumer needs, driven by the growing demand for high-definition video quality and compatibility across diverse devices in both commercial and residential markets.</p></p>
