<p><strong>The Four Way Reversing Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slide-type</li><li>Poppet-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Four-way reversing valves are crucial components in HVAC and refrigeration systems, directing refrigerant flow for heating and cooling. There are two main types: slide-type and poppet-type. </p><p>Slide-type valves operate using a sliding mechanism that shifts the valve position to change the refrigerant direction smoothly. Poppet-type valves use a series of spring-loaded discs that open and close to control flow, offering more compact designs. Each type provides distinct advantages, influencing their applications based on efficiency, space, and reliability requirements.</p></p>
