<p><ul><li>Low Expansion Foam Extinguishing System</li><li>Medium Expansion Foam Extinguishing System</li><li>High Expansion Foam Fire Extinguishing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam fire suppression systems are categorized into three main types based on their expansion ratios. Low expansion foam systems create a thick blanket of foam, ideal for suppressing flammable liquid fires and preventing re-ignition. Medium expansion foam systems generate a balanced foam density suitable for both liquid and solid fires, offering versatile protection. High expansion foam systems produce a vast volume of foam that rapidly fills spaces, effectively smothering fires in enclosed areas, such as warehouses and aircraft hangars.</p></p>
