<p><strong>The Green Ammonia Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline Water Electrolysis</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green ammonia market includes three primary electrolysis technologies for hydrogen production: </p><p>1. **Alkaline Water Electrolysis** utilizes liquid electrolytes and offers a mature, cost-effective solution, suitable for large-scale applications. </p><p>2. **Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolysis** features solid polymer electrolytes, providing higher efficiency and rapid response, making it ideal for variable renewable energy sources.</p><p>3. **Solid Oxide Electrolysis** employs solid ceramic materials, enabling high-temperature operation and enhanced efficiency, particularly in integrated energy systems. Each type contributes uniquely to sustainable ammonia production.</p></p>
