<p><p>Mobile payment technologies can be categorized into two main types: proximity payments and remote payments. Proximity payments involve transactions conducted in close physical proximity, typically using technologies like NFC (Near Field Communication) or QR codes, allowing users to make instant payments at retail locations. In contrast, remote payments enable transactions from a distance, often through mobile apps or websites, facilitating purchases without the need for physical interaction. Both types enhance convenience and streamline the payment process in different contexts.</p></p>
