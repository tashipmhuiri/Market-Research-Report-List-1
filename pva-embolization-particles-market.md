<p><p>The PVA embolization particles market is segmented into three main types: conventional/irregular PVA, compressible PVA, and spherical PVA. Conventional/irregular PVA particles are versatile and commonly used for various embolization procedures due to their pliability. Compressible PVA particles can easily adapt to the vascular structure, enhancing flow control. Spherical PVA particles are designed for consistent size and flow characteristics, providing precise occlusion. Each type serves specific clinical needs, contributing to the advancement of interventional radiology techniques.</p></p>
