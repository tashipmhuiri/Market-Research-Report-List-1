<p><p>The intra-uterine contraceptive devices (IUCD) market comprises primarily two types: Copper IUCDs and Hormonal IUCDs. Copper IUCDs, infused with copper, create an inhospitable environment for sperm, providing effective long-term contraception without hormones. Hormonal IUCDs, on the other hand, release synthetic hormones like progestin to thicken cervical mucus and thin the uterine lining, enhancing contraceptive effectiveness while also offering additional benefits like reduced menstrual bleeding. Both types cater to diverse preferences and health needs within the contraceptive landscape.</p></p>
