<p><p>The programmable power supply device market is categorized into three main types: single-output, dual-output, and multiple-output types. Single-output types provide a single voltage and current output, ideal for straightforward applications. Dual-output types offer two independent outputs, allowing for more flexibility in powering devices with differing requirements. Multiple-output types can deliver several outputs simultaneously, catering to complex systems that require various voltages and currents. Each type serves distinct market needs, from basic testing to intricate industrial applications.</p></p>
