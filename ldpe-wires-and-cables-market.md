<p><p>The LDPE wires and cables market includes various types, primarily focusing on LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) and XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene). LLDPE offers enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring durability in challenging environments. XLPE, on the other hand, is known for its superior thermal and electrical properties, making it ideal for high-voltage applications and improved safety.
